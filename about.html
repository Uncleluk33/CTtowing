ABOUT C&T TOWING

We're not a call center or a national chain. We're a local towing company built by someone who knows these roads, knows this community, and shows up every time.

Our Story

LOCAL ROOTS, REAL SERVICE

C&T Towing & Roadside was built on a simple idea: when someone is stranded on the side of the road, they deserve fast, honest help from someone who actually cares.

Based in Reisterstown, MD, we serve the communities we live in — Owings Mills, Pikesville, Randallstown, Westminster, Eldersburg, and throughout Baltimore County and Carroll County. When you call us, you're talking to someone local who will be on their way in minutes, not hours.

With over 10 years of experience and more than 70 five-star Google reviews, we've built our reputation one tow at a time. We take pride in doing the job right — whether it's a quick jump start or a long-distance flatbed haul.
