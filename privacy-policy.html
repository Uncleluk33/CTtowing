PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

C&T Towing & Roadside ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website cttowingmd.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, phone number, and email address when you contact us directly.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on the site, browser type, and referring URLs, collected automatically via Google Analytics and Google Ads tracking.
  • Location data — approximate location inferred from your IP address, used to provide relevant local service information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to service requests and dispatch towing or roadside assistance.
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.
  • To contact you regarding your inquiry or service.

Google Analytics & Advertising

This site uses Google Analytics and Google Ads conversion tracking (tag ID: AW-18107258551). These tools collect anonymized data about site visits and may use cookies to track user behavior across sessions. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Note that some parts of the site may not function properly without cookies.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide our services (e.g., dispatching) or as required by law. We may use third-party tools such as Google for analytics and advertising purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information you transmit to us.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
