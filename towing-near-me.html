Towing Services

WE TOW EVERYTHING

Whether you're stranded on I-695, I-795, I-70, MD 140 or a local road, C&T Towing & Roadside has you covered. Our fleet of tow trucks is ready to respond to any towing situation.

  • Emergency Towing — Broken down on the highway or side street, we respond fast to get you off the road safely.
  • Accident Towing — We work directly with insurance companies and will tow your vehicle from the accident scene.
  • Flatbed Towing — Safe, damage-free transport for all vehicle types including low-clearance and all-wheel drive vehicles.
  • Long Distance Towing — Need to move a vehicle out of state or across Maryland? We handle long distance transport.
  • Winch Out Service — Stuck off the road? Our operators have the equipment to recover your vehicle safely.
  • 24/7 Availability — Day or night, weekends, holidays — we're always here when you need us.
